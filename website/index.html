About

Presenting 'DialEd' a revolutionary tech-education initiative for remote Indian villages. With a unique calling system, users can access comprehensive learning resources without internet. This groundbreaking project empowers learners by providing instant answers to queries, fostering education in areas with limited connectivity. ConnectEduVoice aims to break barriers, making quality education accessible to all, transforming lives in the remotest corners of India.
